Vocabulary Booster

Boost your vocabulary by playing this game! You will be shown 8 words in your selected language and 6 words in your target language, and your goal is to match the words correctly!

Touchscreen: Match the words by tapping first your selected option and then one of the empty answer squares.


Mouse: If you are using a mouse, click on your selected option and drag and drop it to one of the empty answer squares.
