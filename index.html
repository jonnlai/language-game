Vocabulary Booster

Boost your vocabulary by playing this game! You will be shown 8 words in your selected language and 6 words in your target language, and your goal is to match the words correctly!

Touchscreen: If you are playig using a touchscreen device, match the words by tapping first your selected option and then one of the empty answer squares.

Mouse: If you are using a mouse, click your selected option and drag and drop it to one of the empty answer squares.

Select your prefered language combination and theme, and click play!
